Towson

  • About Towson: Towson University is a college located in Baltimore County at the following address, 8000 York Rd, Towson, MD 21252. It is the second largest university in the state of Maryland. The university enrolled 22,284 students as of 2015. Also, Towson prides itself on being a clean, smoke free campus. Towson offers different kinds of majors and programs to study such as Accounting, Biology, and Information Systems, along with many other choices depending on a student's goals and interests.
  • Campus Life: Towson prides itself on being a clean, smoke free campus. The campus has different waste options for the students and faculty in the dining halls with the ability to trash, recycle, and compost food waste. At Towson, there are many recreational activities which include the clubs on campus
  • Construction Projects: Towson has many ongoing and completed construction and expansion projects to help improve the university. One of them is a new redesigned student union that comes equipped with additional rooms and more space for student activities. One of the projects they have completed is the expansion of their campus gym to provide a state of the art fitness facility.
