Crofton

  • My Home Town: I have lived in Crofton for my entire life. I attended Crofton Elementary School, Crofton Middle School, and Arundel High School. From my perspective, Crofton was a good place to grow up and it has a very family oriented feel. In Crofton, there are many different options as far as schools, restaurants, and other kinds of services.
  • Activities: There are many places to go in Crofton for recreation. Crofton also offers an abundance of commercial stores and buildings. I happen to live close to a library, some shopping centers, a gym, and a golf course. Crofton is also located close to Annapolis, Baltimore, and Washington, DC which have many different things to do.
  • Recent Developments: The city has changed a lot since I was younger, with many projects and expansions currently being done. Starting in the fall of 2020, Crofton residents will be able to attend the newly built high school, known as Crofton High School. Crofton has built two new senior care facilities and a new shopping center on Main Chapel Way.
